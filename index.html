I’m a creative technologist. I like to discover technology in depth and translate its potential to engaging and meaningful experiences, products and services.

I’ve been playing with software and hardware since I was a kid and have more than 7 years of professional experience, most of which I spent in the creative industry. I enjoy leading the development of tech projects but never stopped making things myself because I find it inspiring to learn new things this way and the process of solving technical problems always generate interesting new ideas. Also, I believe that this is the only way to maintain a detailed understanding of what is possible and where the challenges lay.

I am lucky to work with a lot of talented people at BBH London and BBH Labs.

My job is to improve the quality of the creative output by:
  • Helping the agency harness new technologies and creative solutions
  • Developing ideas and concepts, in partnership with creatives
  • Building prototypes and overseeing the delivery of technically-challenging projects
