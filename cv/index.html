As lead creative technologist in the agency, my job is to improve the quality of the creative output by:

  • Helping the agency harness new technologies and creative solutions;
  • Developing ideas and concepts, in partnership with creatives;
  • Building prototypes and overseeing the delivery of technically-challenging projects.

My ideas and research played a determining part in many of our recent pitch wins, including Virgin Media and Huawei. Recent live projects include: Home for Christmas, a collaboration with Barnardos for Christmas 2012, Johnnie Walker Progressive Timeline, the launch of the brand on Instagram, British Airways Taxi, a customisable video for the Don’t Fly campaign, Champion of the Playground game for Robinsons and Vidazzl with BBH Labs.

Webkombinat, Budapest, Creative Developer – June 2007 – October 2008

I was responsible for delivering whole projects alone or with a small team, directly communicating with the clients and I acquired a lot of practical knowledge in concepting, developing and maintaining complex online solutions.
