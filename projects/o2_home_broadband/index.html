O2 Home Broadband - the Great Niggle Noble

  • Campaign video
  • Creative Brand Advertiser Award ‘10’
  • Creative Showcase Winner ‘10 July’
  • FWA Mobile Of The Day 9th September 2010

This was part of an O2 campaign aiming to show customers that O2 had recognized all their frustrations concerning home broadband, and were determined to put an end to all these niggles.

My last big Flash project at Agency Republic. I built the whole Flash front-end using Robotlegs, AS-Signals and the Google Maps Flash API. The 3D models were relatively complex and when the 3D guy couldnâ€™t reduce the polygon-count further I optimised the Away3D rendering pipeline to make it faster. I used Pixelbender to add depth of field to the map texture in the throwing part. It was also interesting to polish the AI of the chasing part because the steering of the game characters had to be natural, they had to detect and avoid water on the map (and avoid getting stuck in peninsulas), run away from the hand that chased them but not to go too far so the player still had a chance to catch all of them in time.
