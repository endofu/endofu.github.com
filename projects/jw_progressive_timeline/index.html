Johnnie Walker Progressive Timeline

Writeup by Edelman

Johnnie Walker stands for progress, but as a brand it tends to behave quite conservatively. This project was about demonstrating what the brand promise could mean in the digital and social space. We created the first Progressive Timeline Cover, enabling Johnnie Walker’s Timeline to evolve and reflect users’ visions of progress as captured on Instagram.

I designed and built the whole solution that displays slideshows in the place of the Johnnie Walker Timeline’s cover image. The admin system allows the global social management team to gather permissions from the different local JW Facebook pages’ admins (almost a hundred worldwide) and manage their cover ‘slideshows’ from one place. It collects photos from Instagram using different search parameters (uploader username, hashtag, uploading time), moderating and comping them into groups of 4 Instagrams with a selected background frame to create sequences of ‘slides’ for the slideshow. It was built in Django using PIL, lives on Google App Engine.
